Welcome to the battle arena!

Aims at showing how chosing a specfic value for a feature will impact on another statistic. With this visualization, explore the whole Pokémon population generation-wise. Choose the generation you're interested in with the left drop down menu and then sort out the Pokémons of that generation according to a certain feature chosen with the right drop down menu. Then you can put your mouse over the dots around the Pokéball to display an ID card of the corresponding Pokémon which contains a picture and a spider chart with its stats. When you find a Pokémon to your liking and want more informations on it or if you are curious about how strong a certain Pokémon is, you can click on the dot: a more complete information sheet will appear and the outcome of every battles will be displayed. Upon clicking, the in-game cry of the Pokémon will be played. To turn that sound on and off, click on the sound icon.
